Welcome to the Losers' club

Bill Denbrough

William "Bill" Denbrough is the leader of the Losers' Club. He is the eldest son of Zack and Sharon Denbrough, as well as the older brother of the late George Denbrough.

Richie Tozier

Richie is the comic of the group; however, his loudmouthed antics often get him in trouble, leading to him being called "Richie 'Trashmouth' Tozier" and his friends often using the phrase "Beep Beep Richie" when they want him to be quiet.

Eddie Kaspbrak

Eddie is the physically most fragile member of the group; is an asthmatic and hypochondriac. His father died when he was very young, and his mother, a domineering and overprotective woman, has Munchausen syndrome by proxy.

Beverly Marsh

Bev is an attractive and tomboyish redhead with whom each of the boys has a secret crush at some point in history. She is the only girl in the group and comes from the poorest part of Derry. Her mother died when she was a child and she has a difficult childhood due to her abusive father.

Benjamin Hanscom

Ben, known as "Haystack" by his friends, is a very intelligent boy who, before joining the group, often spent his free time reading books in the library. Due to childhood obesity, he was the victim of constant bulling. His mechanical skills are useful to Losers.

Stanley Uris

Stan is the most skeptical member of the Club. He is Jewish and is persecuted for this reason. Logic, order, and cleanliness are deeply ingrained in his psyche. He relies on logic more than anything else and is the least willing to accept that It actually exists.

Mike Hanlon

Mike is the last to join the Losers. He is the only African-American in the group and lives with his grandparents on a farm. He goes to a different school from the other kids. Mike is racially persecuted.
